# SALA 2026

**Event:** [SALA '26](https://lasala.ai/), Quito, Ecuador
**Date:** 11 March 2026
**Format:** ~60 minute talk (with Q&A)

## Talk: An Introduction to RLHF and Post-training

A self-contained introduction aimed at an ML-literate audience that may not be deeply familiar with post-training or RLHF. Covers the trajectory from classical RL to modern language-model alignment, what post-training actually changes, key papers, and the distinction between RLHF, RLVR, and classical RL.
