% Distillation directionality: Sequence-KD vs OPD vs OPSD
% Compares, left to right, WHO generates the rollout and WHERE supervision flows:
%   Seq KD : teacher generates offline; cross-entropy pushes the student to match
%   OPD    : student generates on-policy (RL); teacher supervises per token (reverse KL)
%   OPSD   : ONE model in two roles -- a teacher conditioned on an inserted hint
%            (privileged info added to its context) and a student with the original
%            context; a localized reverse-KL pulls the student (no hint) toward the
%            hinted teacher, so it learns to behave well without the hint at inference.
% Mockup for iteration -- not pixel perfect.
